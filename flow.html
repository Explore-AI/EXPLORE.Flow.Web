What is Flow?

When you’re ‘in the zone’, ‘killing it’ or ‘crushing things’ - that is flow.

Flow is a state of absolute absorption in a task, an optimal experience, when you’re fully immersed in something: it is when everything clicks into place and you effortlessly perform at your peak.

The great athletes, musicians, artists and scientists recorded long periods of intense flow. The great achievements and records of humanity would not be possible without it.

Flow is real. We all know it. We all have experienced it. The question is: how do we achieve it more often and more deeply in our day to day lives.
